Lambda Labs - Full Stack

Delphe is an app designed to connect beginners and novices with experts in their topic of choice. The app is designed with two user types in mind, the expert and the asker. Askers can post questions to the app and label them with a topic. Experts can then answer those questions from their dashboard. Hollywood Blacklist Analysis

Over 300 Blacklist screenplays have been made into feature films. Those films have earned over $26 billion at the worldwide box office, have been nominated for 264 Academy Awards, and have won 48 of them, including Best Pictures SLUMDOG MILLIONAIRE, THE KING'S SPEECH, ARGO and SPOTLIGHT. They've also won ten of the last twenty screenwriting Oscars. In this project I work through a dataset of Blacklist movies and descriptions along with a few other attributes to gather insights and try my hand at parsing text data. Find out more in the README summary.
